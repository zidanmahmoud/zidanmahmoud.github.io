Biography

I am a PhD Student at the Associate Professorship of Computational Mechanics at the Technical University of Munich, part of a group working on Multi-fidelity Failure Modeling and Optimization for Natural Fiber Structures in Complex Environments, funded by the International Graduate School of Science and Engineering (IGSSE). This project is currently aimed to be implemented in the open-source multiphysics Finite Element framework, Kratos.
